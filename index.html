F.A.Q

  • What are the dates and registration fees for Hackathon ?

    The event will be organized on 1st, 2nd and 3rd October. There is no registration fees for the event but Rs.100 per team has to be submitted while registering as an assurance amount which will be returned to the team on 1st October.

  • Do I need to have any specific qualifications to be a participant for the Hackathon?

    You love to code, you are more than welcome to participate in the Hackathon.

  • What are the accomodation facilities and will travel allowances be provided ?

    Accommodation will be provided to all the teams coming from outside Pune.

  • How to reach the location of hackathon?

    The hackathon will be conducted in our college itself - you can refer the map at the Event Venue section of the page.
